Cards

Cards are flexible containers that group related content and actions. They provide a consistent way to present information across the interface.

Usage

<div class="card card--basic">
    <h3 class="card__title">Card Title</h3>
    <p class="card__description">Card description text</p>
    <div class="card__footer">
        <span class="card__meta">Meta information</span>
        <button class="button button--secondary">Action</button>
    </div>
</div>

Best Practices

  • Keep card content concise and focused
  • Use consistent padding and spacing
  • Include clear calls-to-action when needed
  • Maintain hierarchy within card content
  • Use appropriate hover states for interactive cards
  • Optimize images for performance
